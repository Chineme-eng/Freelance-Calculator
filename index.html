How to charge without scaring clients away
01
Never price from desperation
Your rate should come from your income target, capacity, and business costs — not random emotion in a DM.
02
Hourly is your floor, not always your offer
Use the calculator to find your true hourly minimum, then package work as project pricing for better upside.
03
Premium clients buy confidence
Higher prices feel more justified when your scope is clear, your process is smooth, and your positioning looks mature.
04
Retainers smooth your income
One-off projects can work, but monthly retainers make your business more stable and easier to scale.
